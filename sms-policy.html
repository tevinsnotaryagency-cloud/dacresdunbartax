SMS Messaging Policy

How we use text messaging to communicate with clients

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  |  Dacres & Dunbar Tax Service

Overview

Dacres & Dunbar Tax Service may use SMS (text message) communications to stay in touch with clients. This policy explains how our SMS program works and your rights as a recipient.

Types of Messages

  • Appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Service updates and status notifications
  • Requests for documents or information needed to complete your return
  • Customer support responses

How to Opt In

You may opt in to receive SMS messages by checking the SMS consent checkbox on our contact form and submitting your phone number. Consent is entirely voluntary and not required to use our services.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any SMS message to unsubscribe immediately. You will receive a confirmation, then no further messages will be sent. Reply HELP for assistance, or contact us at (954) 895-2868 or [email protected].

Message Frequency & Rates

Message frequency varies based on your service activity and appointment schedule. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Carrier Disclaimer

Mobile carriers are not responsible for delayed or undelivered messages. Delivery is subject to carrier availability.

Privacy

Your mobile number will never be shared with or sold to third parties for marketing purposes. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Eligibility

SMS communications are available to clients who are 18 years of age or older and have a valid U.S. mobile phone number.
